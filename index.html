Welcome to Fantasy Olympian

Create the ultimate fantasy Olympic team and cheer on your athletes in Paris this summer!

How It Works

Draft 10 athletes to your team; consult our athlete rankings by sport found here.

Your team must consist of three swimmers/divers, three track and field athletes, one weightlifter/wrestler, and three other athletes.

Complete trades and make substitutions to your lineup before your athletes' events start.

Track your points and see who is crowned champion in your group at the end!
